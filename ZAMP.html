Our Approach

Simply engineering various solutions to monogamous problems has only achieved a fraction of the outcome we'd expected. As we work to implement scientific lenses into ZAMP, our vision for the future of Zambia and Africa has improved to not just improving the conditions of today, but educating youth to act on tomorrows' behalf.

Our Approach

Our Approach

At ZAMP, we take a holistic approach to environmental advocacy. We've worked directly with members of the Zambian government to develop facilities for farmer empowerment and food processing. Furthermore, we are currently mentoring 15 graduate students on topics of agricultural innovation, working to solves the problems of today and educating more to take action in solving the problems of tomorrow.

Our Impact

Our Approach

Our Impact

Our impacts are defined with 2 lenses- first, we've built wells in partnership with IAPRI, helping provide water to over 75,000 citizens in surrounding regions. Second, we're creating low-cost soil sensors for over 3000 farmers, indicating soil pH and moisture contents to improve agricultural techniques. Overall, we've raised over $2,000 to accomplish this.
